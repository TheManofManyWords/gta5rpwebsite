Question 1: What is Los Santos Roleplay?
Los Santos Roleplay is a community where players come together to roleplay scenarios in the world of GTA V.
Question 2: How do I join?
In order to join Society as a civilian, you must register to the #cad channel in our discord (link in the Contact page) using our code. Then, you must register your vehicle, guns, and so on, with the cad. Once done, you may now register your weapons, and vehicles, in ⁠#vehicle-register, and ⁠#register-weapons. After that, you must wait till a session is hosted, and simply attend, and listen to the Session Host. With your help, Society will become one of the best, Xbox Roleplay servers.
Question 3: How do I become an officer?
To become a member of the Los Santos Police Department, head over to the Applications page and apply to be a member of the LSPD! You will be notified if you are accepted/denied in the discord.
